Oskar Schindler

The hero of the Holocaust

Here's a timeline of Oskar Schindler's life:

  • 1908 - Born in Zwittau, Austria-Hungary.
  • 1936 - Schindler joined the military inteligence service of Nazy Germany.
  • 1939 - He aquired an enamelware factory in Krakow, Poland and employed 1000 Jews.
  • 1944 - Moves his factory to Brenec, thus sparing his jewish workers from death in the concentration camps.
  • 1945 - After World War II, Schindler moved to West Germany and to Argentina afterwords where he and his family took up farming.
  • 1958 - After going bankrupt, he left his wife and returned to Germany and failed at several business ventures.
  • 1974 - Dies aged 66 in Hildesheim, West Germany
  • 1993 - Schindler and his wife Emilie were named Righteous Among the Nations by the Israeli government.

You can find out more about Schindler and his life on his wikipedia entry.
